Course Content

Each course consists of three things

  1. vedio lessons: High quality pre-recorded sessions that you can watch anywhere, anytime, at your own pace. You can rewatch it as many times as you want to learn better.
  2. Assignments: When you are done with a session, you can read/download the assignment. These assignments come with instructions to make the learning more interactive and hands-on.
  3. Submission: As you follow the step by step instructions in the assignment, your artwork will be complete. You can click a picture of your artwork and submit it to us. We approve your assignment and feature your best work on our Instagram page.

Meet your teacher

India-based artist Mamta Singh is a professional wall artist, doodler and an illustrator. Originally a law graduate, Mamta found her calling in art when she painted a wall in her house. When she completed the wall, she knew she wants to do this for the rest of her life. In 2012, she started by selling handmade products like mugs and diaries. She widened her horizons into wall murals, illustrations and art installations. In 2016, she took up art full-time. By 2018, she was one of the most loved artists in the country. After creating a niche for herself in India, Mamta kicked off her global journey in 2019. On her way to become Asia’s most loved art tutor, Mamta conducted over 300 workshops for 4000+ students across India, Nepal, Philippines and Indonesia. Much like Mamta herself, her workshops are like a breath of fresh air. Her unique style is to teach art through fun. She believes there is in each one of us. She emphasizes on finding your personal style and voice in art. She believes consistency, practice and originality takes each one a very long way. She switches her time between transforming walls of clients and transforming minds of aspirants at her workshops. She loves to travel, ride bikes, play drums and learn something new each day
